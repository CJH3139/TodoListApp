About

skeeebeeedeee is a task management app. Use General for everyday tasks and School for organizing assignments by course.

Guest mode: Use without an account tasks are saved locally in your browser.

With account: Sign in to sync your tasks across all your devices. Supports Discord, GitHub, and Google authentication.

Features: Task priorities, tags, subtasks, recurring tasks, voice input, dark/light themes, and cloud sync.
